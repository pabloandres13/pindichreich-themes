# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

German-speaking mothers — primarily juggling young children, work, and family life. They read on mobile, identify with an honest and relatable voice, and are looking for a blogger who sounds like a real person rather than a brand. They're not looking for perfection; they want recognition and warmth.

## Product Purpose

A German mom-blog (Eltern-/Mama-Blog) built as a WordPress Astra child theme. Covers the full spectrum of family life: everyday chaos, the good moments, parenting, wellbeing, and the things that actually happen at home. Success = a site owner who can publish comfortably through Gutenberg and Customizer in German, with a design that feels personal and warm without being generic.

## Brand Personality

Warm, honest, playful. Run by one real person — not a brand. Three words: herzlich, ehrlich, lebendig (warm, honest, lively). The tone is that of a close friend writing about her actual life: funny when things go sideways, heartfelt when they don't. No hustle culture, no "top 10 tips", no influencer polish.

## Anti-references

- Polished lifestyle magazine aesthetic (too perfect, too curated, too cold)
- Corporate parenting brand (BabyCenter-style: clinical, generic, trust-badge-heavy)
- Pinterest-optimized flat-lay blog (posed, aspirational, emotionally distant)
- English-language mom-blog template translated to German (doesn't fit the voice)

## Design Principles

1. **Personality over production value** — the handwritten script accent (Caveat), rounded Fredoka headings, and coral/teal palette all signal "one person, not a studio". The design should feel made with care, not manufactured.
2. **Non-technical by design** — author name, bio, Instagram handle, and newsletter URL are all Customizer controls. Content lives in Gutenberg blocks and shortcodes. No PHP editing required.
3. **Warm but not saccharine** — coral and teal are energetic and slightly playful, not soft-pink-nursery. The ink near-black text keeps it readable and grounded.
4. **German-first** — all UI strings, Customizer labels, and placeholder copy are in German. The voice and tone conventions follow German blogging norms (du-form, direct, personal).
5. **Mobile-first** — the target reader is on a phone, probably one-handed. Generous tap targets, fluid type scale, sections that stack cleanly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Focus ring uses the coral accent color. `prefers-reduced-motion` disables all animations. Semantic HTML throughout. Font sizes use fluid `clamp()` scales — readable at all viewport widths without zoom.
